# Customer Analytics Insights

## Executive Recommendations

- Prioritize high-value customers with recent activity for loyalty benefits and upsell offers.
- Build a win-back list from customers with strong historic revenue and rising inactivity.
- Use low-frequency customer cohorts for onboarding improvements and second-purchase campaigns.
- Track churn risk at segment level so marketing spend is tied to measurable customer value.

## KPI Layer

- Total revenue
- Average order value
- Order frequency
- Recency in days
- Churn-risk score
- Segment-level revenue contribution
